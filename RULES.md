GAME RULES
==========


SNAKE AND FRUIT
----------------
- Move using W, A, S, D
- Eat fruit to increase score
- Do not hit walls or your own tail


TIC TAC TOE
-----------
- Player is O, Bot is X
- Enter row and column (1 to 3)
- Bot tries to win or block
- Game ends on win or draw


MINESWEEPER
-----------
- R = Reveal
- F = Flag
- B = Back to menu
- Revealing a mine ends the game
- Flag all 10 mines to win
